2015 MerCruiser 4.3 TKS Timing Check Procedure
(Service Manual #32)

References:

Section 1C, Page 1C-4 – Engine Specifications
Section 4B, Page 4B-2 – Timing Light (Special Tool)
Section 4B, Page 4B-21 – Thunderbolt V Models Timing
Section 4B, Pages 4B-13 through 4B-15 – Distributor Installation

Specifications:

Idle Speed: 600 RPM (Neutral)
Base Timing: 10° BTDC
Firing Order: 1-6-5-4-3-2
WOT RPM: 4400-4800

Procedure:

Warm engine to normal operating temperature.
Verify idle speed is approximately 600 RPM in neutral.
Hook timing light to:
#1 spark plug wire
Battery +
Battery -
Highlight the timing mark and pointer with white paint or chalk.
Start engine and observe timing.
BEFORE MOVING ANYTHING:
Record timing value.
Take a picture of timing mark if possible.
IMPORTANT:
Read Service Manual #32, Page 4B-21 (Thunderbolt V Models Timing).
Verify whether the ignition module must be placed into Base Timing Mode before checking or adjusting timing.
Mercury references Base Timing Mode for Thunderbolt V ignition systems elsewhere in the service information.
DO NOT adjust distributor timing until this procedure has been verified.
If timing is NOT 10° BTDC:
Follow the Base Timing Mode procedure from Page 4B-21.
Loosen distributor hold-down slightly.
Rotate distributor until timing reads 10° BTDC.
Tighten distributor hold-down (18 lb-ft).
Recheck timing.
If a large distributor movement is required:
Verify distributor installation.
Bring #1 cylinder to TDC compression.
Confirm distributor indexing per Pages 4B-13 through 4B-15.
Verify distributor is not installed one tooth off.
Water test:
Warm engine.
Gradually accelerate.
Verify smooth power.
Verify WOT RPM 4400-4800.
Listen for pinging or detonation.

Things to verify while there:

Plug wires fully seated
Firing order 1-6-5-4-3-2 clockwise
Carburetor reaches full throttle
Vacuum hoses connected
Distributor cap and rotor in good condition
